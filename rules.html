Rules

  • Group Stage Predictions:
    • 10 points: If both the predicted and actual scores for both teams match exactly.
    • 8 points: If the predicted goal difference and match outcome (win, loss, draw) match the actual result, even if the exact scores do not.
    • 5 points: If the predicted result is a draw, and the actual result is also a draw, regardless of the exact score.
    • 5 points: If the predicted match outcome (win or loss) is correct, but the exact scores or goal difference are incorrect.
    • 0 points: If none of the above conditions are met.
  • Knockout Stage Predictions:
    • Round of 16: 20 points for correctly predicting the winner of a match.
    • Quarterfinals: 40 points for correctly predicting the winner of a match.
    • Semifinals: 60 points for correctly predicting the winner of a match.
    • Final: 80 points for correctly predicting the winner of the final match.
  • Top Players Predictions:
    • 80 points: for correctly predicting a top player in the tournament.

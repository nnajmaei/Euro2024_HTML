Points System

Group Stage Predictions:

  • 10 points: If the exact scoreline is predicted
  • 8 points: If the predicted match outcome (only win or loss) and GD match the actual result, even if the exact scores do not match.
  • 5 points: If the predicted result is a draw, and the actual result is also a draw, regardless of the exact score.
  • 5 points: If the predicted match outcome (win or loss) is correct, but the exact scores or goal difference are incorrect.
  • 0 points: If none of the above conditions are met.

Knockout Stage Predictions:

  • 20 points: for each correctly predicted qualified team to QF
  • 40 points: for each correctly predicted qualified team to SF
  • 60 points: for each correctly predicted qualified team to F
  • 80 points: for correctly predicted Champions

Top Players Predictions:

  • 80 points: for correctly predicting the Top Goal Scorer.
  • 80 points: for correctly predicting the Best Player.
